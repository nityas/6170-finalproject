Feeding MIT, one Byte at a time

Bytemap aims to connect users with on-campus
food offerings in an easy to digest, intuitive way.

Browse the map for bytes near you

Our easy to use map interface allows you to search anywhere on MIT's campus and alerts you to current bytes with dropped pins. You can click on a pin to view all offerings at that location, or if you're in a hurry, view all current offerings using our newsfeed. Just don't forget to click "all gone" if you took the last byte!

Leftover Food? Post a byte of your own

Create an account to post to our site and attract hungry MIT students to take those leftovers off your hands. We're also listening to MIT's free food mailing list, free-food@mit.edu, so send out an email and we'll take care of making it into a Byte!

Get Notified

Subscribe to the buildings you visit most often to receive text notifications when a new Byte has been posted. Never miss out on free food near you again!

Never Leave Disappointed

We clear out old Bytes periodically and leverage the Bytemap community to crowdsource Byte availability, so you'll never get to a Byte only to find that someon else took that last slice of pizza.
